Section 13:Dog clouds小狗云

XiaoJin 小今云

Philadelphia, USA 美国费城 · April 2026 2026年四月 · Common 常见

A special eight-month-old puppy cloud spotted on a spring lawn, shaped like a Cavalier King Charles bichon. Very common around University City Philadelphia and greets every human it meets with great enthusiasm. 在春天的草地上发现的一块特殊的八个月的小狗云，形状似骑士比熊犬， 在费城大学城非常常见，会跟所有路上遇到的人类热情地打招呼。

XiaoBei 小贝云

Nanchang, China 中国南昌 · March 2026 2026年三月 · Somewhat rare 较为少见

A round, five-year-old bichon-shaped cloud who is shy around strangers — patience is required before you're allowed to pet him. 一块胖乎乎的五岁的比熊云，对陌生人类很害羞，需要耐心才能摸到。

Ali 阿力云

Nanchang, China 中国南昌 · March 2026 2026年三月 · Common 常见

A nine-year-old schnauzer-shaped cloud with very firm opinions about which route to walk each day — but a piece of beef will guarantee a successful petting. 对于每天要走什么路线非常有自己想法的一朵九岁的雪纳瑞云， 但只要拿出卤牛肉（少盐）就一定可以摸到。
